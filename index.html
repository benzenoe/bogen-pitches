🚨 Critical Issues (Must Fix Before EU Launch)
CRITICAL
🔒

GDPR & Cookie Compliance

Current site has no GDPR-compliant cookie consent banner. EU law requires explicit opt-in consent before any tracking cookies (Google Analytics, Clarity) are set. Fine risk: up to 4% of global annual revenue under GDPR. Estimated fix: 2–3 days.

CRITICAL
💶

No EUR / GBP Pricing

All pricing currently in Indian Rupees (₹). European buyers will not convert without local currency pricing. Requires currency switcher (GBP, EUR, CHF) with live exchange rate API or fixed European pricing tiers. Blocker for EU sales.

CRITICAL
💳

Missing EU Payment Methods

No Klarna, Stripe, iDEAL, Sofort, Bancontact, or PayPal visible on the checkout. EU buyers expect BNPL options (Klarna/Afterpay) — especially for high-ticket items €500–€2,000. Cart abandonment will be 70%+ without these.

CRITICAL
🚚

No EU Shipping Information

No visible EU shipping policy, estimated delivery times, duties/VAT information, or customs declaration details. European buyers need to know: cost, time, and whether they owe import duty at delivery. Missing info = lost sales.

CRITICAL
📋

No EU Returns Policy

EU Consumer Rights Directive requires minimum 14-day "cooling off" right-to-return on all distance sales. The existing 14-day Indian return policy must be explicitly updated to reference EU statutory rights and be available in EN/FR/DE.

CRITICAL
🌐

No Geo-Detection / Localisation

Site serves all visitors identically regardless of location. No geo-IP detection to route EU visitors to an EU landing page, show local currency, or surface EU-relevant content. First impression for European visitors is an Indian rupee price — immediate trust failure.

⚠️ High Priority (Fix Within 30 Days)
HIGH
🏷️

Product Listings — EU Optimisation

Titles need EU search terms: "Lab Grown Diamond Engagement Ring UK", not just collection names. Meta descriptions missing. No size guides in mm/European ring sizes (UK uses letters, DE/FR use numeric). No material composition EU labelling compliance.

HIGH
📱

Mobile UX Overhaul

Site is functional on mobile but checkout flow has multiple friction points on small screens. EU buyers complete 68% of jewellery purchases on mobile. Checkout must be one-page, thumb-friendly, with Apple Pay / Google Pay as primary CTA.

HIGH
⭐

Third-Party Review Integration

Current 4.6★ is on-site only — zero credibility for EU buyers. Must integrate Trustpilot or Google Reviews immediately. Target 50+ verified EU reviews in Month 1. EU buyers trust external review platforms 4× more than on-site testimonials.

HIGH
🔍

EU VAT Display

EU law requires prices inclusive of VAT to be displayed prominently. Must show "incl. 20% VAT (UK)" or "incl. TVA 20% (FR)" for each product. Failure to do so is a legal violation in all EU member states and the UK.

HIGH
🖼️

Image Quality & Video

Product photography is good but lacks 360° spin views, macro close-ups of the Ashth Cut® faceting, and lifestyle shots with European aesthetic (Nordic lighting, natural backgrounds). EU luxury buyers expect 8–12 images per product + video.

HIGH
🔐

Trust Badges & Security

No visible SSL badges, payment security icons, or certifications on the checkout page. EU buyers are highly sensitive to checkout security signals. Add: "Secured by SSL", payment method icons, IGI certification badge, and BIS hallmark logos.
